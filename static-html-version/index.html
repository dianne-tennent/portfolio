Madlibs - a game for exploring English grammar

I wanted to make this app because I'd often used Madlibs as a way to reinforce learning parts of speech in the classroom, but it would always be a hassle to print out a bunch of pages and the stories to choose from were fairly limited.

With this app, you can add custom text to use as a Madlib, so teachers and students can either select or write their own stories to use.
